Alex Matzkin, COO

Alex Matzkin is a native of Tampa, Florida and a graduate of the University of Miami. After four years in the medical device field with Nuvasive Spine, he decided to enter the cannabis industry. In 2018, Alex founded SoCal Labs and California Distillate Company in Cathedral City, California. In less than a year, his company grew into one of the largest wholesale manufacturers of cannabis goods in California. Matzkin takes great pride in having the most streamlined and efficient manufacturing processes in the industry. He has since implemented the same system for multiple cannabis and CBD facilities across the United States. With his experience and expertise, Courier Labs will carry out these same manufacturing processes that have been proven to be successful time and time again. This opportunity to be one of Louisiana`s Exclusive manufacturers of CBD products truly excites and inspires him to set the standards of manufacturing and quality above what is expected.

Timmy Thompson CEO

Mr. Thompson and his family members are the lead investors in Cross Creek and its affiliated funds. Mr. Thompson is a member of the Board of Directors of The Cross Group, an offshore oilfield services provider. He has been associated with the company since its inception in 1997, serving in various capacities, including as Senior Vice President of Business Development. Mr. Thompson is also a principal shareholder in The Investment Group, Inc., a privately owned investment company with ownership interests in operating companies, real estate, and other capital ventures. Previously, Mr. Thompson served as President of Continental Financial Group, Inc., a home mortgage lender and financial services company. In 2009, Mr. Thompson, along with his son Tyler Thompson, founded CCP with the goal of developing, financing, and producing a strong slate of feature films with “A-list” talent and broad market appeal. To date, Cross Creek has produced and financed 12 films and has grossed over $1 billion at the worldwide box office.

Wayne P. Theriot, CFO

About Us

California Distillate Company opened the doors to its facility in Cathedral City under SoCal Labs in April 2018. Founder, Alex Matzkin and his team went to work collaborating with cannabis experts from the west coast to create one of the industries largest wholesale manufacturing companies. SoCal Labs offers a variety of core competencies in the cannabis space such as, wholesale distillate, crude, and toll processing. With one of the largest capacities in California, SoCal labs has been a reliable source for top shelf distillate to the premiere brands in the space. SoCal labs prides itself on its clean products and consistency which has been paramount in their success.
